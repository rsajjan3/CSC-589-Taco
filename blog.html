Changes To the Friend’s Collection Coming Soon

By: The Friend Zone Team

November 18, 2018

Changes To The Friend’s Collection Coming Soon

Starting January 30th, 2019, we’ll make some small changes to the friend’s collection we are offering. These follow changes made earlier this year, which update our service terms to match the different world government system.

Specifically, you might notice new friends introduced that match the culture and styling of the area our services are introduced in and offered. These changes will take place in both our web and mobile (spoiler oops) screens. The changes will start to take effect on January 30th, 2019 and may take up to four weeks to reach all users.

Welcome To The Friend Zone

By: The Friend Zone Team

October 26, 2018

Welcome To The Friend Zone

As a human being walking in this world. You have a lot to do. Work to manage. Life to plan.
In other words, you have no time to make friends.

Sure, you do your best to “socialize” (because that’s what you been told to do).
But you’re not happy with the results. Meanwhile, your family and other people who know you want you to start doing so.

“How can I SOLVE this,” you ask.
You being in this site is a good start.

We’ve put together tons and tons of different friends that cover and cut the steps needed in the creation process of the so called "Friends".
Plus, we’ll walk through how to enhance the use of our service.

By the time you’re done, you’ll know exactly how to "Friend" effectively, without spending nearly as much time as others.
The days of sitting alone are over. Stay tuned for more to come.
